    About Me

    Besides the genres of games I enjoy, I typically like to play any game with action, blood, gore, violence, good story, and possibly a good multiplayer. My favorite games include Friday the 13th: The Game, Aliens vs. Predator (2010), Dead by Daylight, Mortal Kombat (2011), Mortal Kombat X, Mortal Kombat 11, Injustice 1 and 2, The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim, the Gears of War series, Halo series, the God of War series, The Last of Us, For Honor, and The Darkness series. I personally invested into the Friday the 13th: The Game's Kickstarter and Backerkit since its inception and had my name included in the game's credits under the special thanks section.
